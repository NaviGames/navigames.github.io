Assassins

"Better move fast in the game of Assassins! Each player is assigned to target another player's base location (the location of the target player at the time the assignments are made). Follow your compass to find your destination. If you reach your target's base location before someone else gets to yours, then your target player is out, and you are now assigned to whoever your target player was targeting before. But move fast, because if someone else reaches your base location first, you're out! Whenever any player gets out, everyone's base location updates to their current location, and the process starts again. The last person standing wins!"

Number of Players: Exactly 4

Swap!

"Ready, set, swap! The rules are simple for this 2 player game: follow your compass towards your opponent's starting location. The first player to reach their target wins!"

Number of Players: Exactly 2

***ALL GAMES ARE PLAYED AT YOUR OWN RISK***
